Our Technology

We don't just build solutions; we build the foundation for them. Our integrated platform provides the tools, security, and scalability to transform your ideas into reality, faster and more efficiently.

Lipid Modification

Protein Engineering tool to lipidate proteins for wide range of applications like increased sensitivity in immunodiagnostics, efficient enzyme/protein immobilization and role as an adjuvant in production of high titers of antibodies.

RAR-ELISA

Developing Rapid and affordable ELISA test kits using in-house technologies and products such as Lipid modification and Coating buffer respectively for high sensitive and specific detection of antigens/antibodies.

IntPCR

One-Step Intercepting PCR (IntPCR) is an advanced molecular amplification method that integrates reverse transcription and nucleic acid differentiation in a single reaction, enabling simultaneous detection of wild-type and mutant sequences.
