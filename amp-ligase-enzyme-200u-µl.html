Amp Ligase Enzyme (5U/µL )

  1. Achieve Precise DNA and RNA Ligation

Amp Ligase enzyme engineered for the robust and highly specific and thermostable NADH-dependent ligation of Sticky ends double stranded DNA.Derived from thermophilic bacteria, this enzyme offers superior performance in a range of molecular biology applications, ensuring efficient and accurate ligation under various conditions.

Key Features

High Efficiency

  • Promotes swift ligation of sticky ends of double stranded DNA.

Thermostability

  • Maintains activity and enhances the performance even at high temperatures up to 95°C

Versatility

  • Ideal for diverse applications such as quantitative PCR, isothermal amplification assays, cloning, and next-generation sequencing (NGS).
